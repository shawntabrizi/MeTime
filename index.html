Have you been craving for more MeTime?

In the workplace there is the struggling conflict between time used to meet with others, and time used to independently work and be productive. MeTime was created by people who wanted to solve this problem in their own lives.

MeTime automatically keeps track of your calendar, and allows you to set a limit on the number of meetings you receive. Once you have reached your preferred limit, MeTime will automatically book off the rest of your day.

FEATURES

Built in the Cloud

MeTime is a cloud service that will work with your Exchange Online calander no matter what device you use to access it.

Configured for You

MeTime allows you to adjust a number of basic and advance settings which will make it the perfect tool no matter what your calander currently looks like.

Simple to Use

MeTime takes less than 1 minute to set up with your calander.

Totally Free
